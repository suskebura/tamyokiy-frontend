📦 Create a Shipment

Start by creating a shipment from your dashboard. Enter sender and receiver details, package weight, and choose your service type.

📍 Track Your Shipment

Every shipment gets a unique tracking number. Use it to track your package in real-time from pickup to delivery.

🔍 Tracking Statuses:

  • 📋 Pending - Awaiting pickup
  • 📦 Picked Up - Package with driver
  • 🚚 In Transit - On the way
  • 🚛 Out for Delivery - Almost there!
  • ✅ Delivered - Package received

🗺️ What is a Route?

A Route is a planned delivery path that a driver follows to deliver multiple packages.

🚚 How Routes Work:

  • Admin creates routes by grouping shipments
  • Driver follows the route to deliver packages
  • You (Client) don't need to worry about routes - just track your shipment!
  • Your shipment status updates automatically as the driver completes each stop

You don't need to create routes - that's done by our admin team. Just create shipments and track them!

Frequently Asked Questions

📦 How do I create a shipment?

Go to Dashboard → Fill in sender/receiver details → Enter weight → Select service → Click "Create Shipment"

📍 How do I track my package?

Use your tracking number on the Track page or view it in your Dashboard under "My Shipments"

🏢 How do I use warehouse storage?

Ship items to our warehouse. They'll appear in "My Storage" where you can view, track, and request dispatch

🚚 How do I request dispatch?

Go to "My Storage" → Click "Request Dispatch" on your item → Enter delivery address → Confirm

💳 What payment methods are accepted?

We accept Credit Card, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and PayPal. All payments are secure.

📄 How do I get an invoice?

Invoices are auto-generated for each shipment. View them in your Dashboard under "My Invoices"
